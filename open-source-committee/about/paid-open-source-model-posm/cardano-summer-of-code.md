**Decision on Cardano Summer of Code Program**\
Following the Open Source Committee (OSC) meeting on November 6, 2025, the committee has decided not to proceed with the Cardano Summer of Code program. This determination was based on several key factors:

* Resource Constraints: Limited availability of personnel and support to manage the initiative effectively.
* Scope of Work: The program's requirements exceeded current capacity and could jeopardize quality.
* Success Criteria: Established benchmarks for program viability and impact, present a barrier too high for success.

While this program will not move forward in the immediate term, it may be revisited as a potential budget proposal for 2026.

**Focus on Existing Programs**\
With nine other active programs currently underway, the OSC prioritizes the effective implementation of initiatives under the Paid Open Source Model (POSM). This strategic focus ensures sustained progress in core areas without overextension.
